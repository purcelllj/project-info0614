About

My name is Lindsey. I started Spübzi Massage in 2004 as a private practice, specializing in massage therapy and relaxation techniques. We wholeheartedly believe in the healing touch here at Spübzi I have always wanted to have vital role in helping people see their concept of a healthy lifestyle become a daily reality. Because of this, I was attending medical school in 2000 in hopes of becoming a doctor. It was in the midst of my schooling that I realized that I was meant to be a massage therapist.

So, here we are! If you are looking to add a little something extra to your life come in today! You deserve to make time to pamper yourself while unwinding in some of the world's most serene settings. Want to indulge in a day, or even a week, of bliss and enjoy natural products that will invigorate, relax, and purify both mind and body? Our best massage solons feature top-rate facilities, with an array of nourishing treatments, healthy activities, and even nutritious gourmet meals available to make sure that you have an amazing and relaxing experience that you won't soon forget. Come visit Spubzi to experience the unforgettable.








Services


Hot Stone

We know you'll love Hot Stone massages. Hot stones are traditionally used as a relaxation method that causes the skin to heat up, facilitating better blood flow. This causes your muscles to release toxins and further relax during your session.













Float Tank

Isolating yourself into a tank of warm water for peace and relaxation. You deserve an hour in our state-of-the-art float tank.








Deep Tissue

Let's face it: Life is tense! What you need is 90 minutes with one of our talented massage therapists. We pride ourselves on whole body health and understand that some of our clients need more work than others!














Swedish Massage

Swedish massage is perfect for soothing back tension. Continuous muscle-warming movements bring you closer to relaxation and relief from any adhesions that may have formed.

In addition to all of these great ways to relax at Spübzi, we offer hot wax hair removal and complementary scalp & hand massage.
